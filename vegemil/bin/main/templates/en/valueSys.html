공유


Dr. Chung’s Food Co., Ltd. is a company that contributes
to human health culture based on the founder's spirit of respect for life,
“Dedicate to human health culture.".

We, Dr. Chung’s Food, promise to become a global food company
that aims for a healthy future for humanity.


Mission

Pursuit of a healthy future for humanity

We contribute to spreading the value of respect for life and a balanced health culture
by providing honest food that protects human health and the global environment

Vision

To become a global food company by 2030

We will become a global food company
that seeks for a healthy future for people all over the world by 2030
