Vegemil Black Bean Soymilk
01 Scientifically designed healthy Soymilk

- It contains various kinds of good nutrition such as protein, essential fatty acids, oligosaccharides and dietary fibers as well as vitamins and minerals.
02 Well-balanced nutrition

- It is nutritious Soymilk designed to help evenly absorb the nutrition of black beans and calcium. Black beans have numerous health benefits such as preventing cancer and boosting nervous system.
03 Promoting calcium absorption

- Calcium to phosphorus ratio is set to be about 2:1 to improve bioavailability of calcium. It also contains an abundant amount of vitamin D3 to help absorb calcium and phosphorus.
※ 1pack(190ml) contains 1.5mg of Vitamin D3 (30% of daily value)
