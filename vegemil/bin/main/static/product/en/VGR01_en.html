Vegemil Almond & Walnut Soymilk
01 Nutty flavor of almonds and walnuts

- By using almonds and walnuts, which contain a sufficient amount of vitamin E and unsaturated fatty acids, you can enjoy rich nutty flavored soymilk.
02 Proper meal replacement

- It is rich in nutrition and it can also be used as a healthy meal replacement.
03 Good for bone health

- By adding calcium, vitamin D3 and dietary fiber to healthy nutrients of soybeans like protein, essential fatty acid and oligosaccharide, it satisfies both taste and nutritional balance.
※ 1 pack (190ml) contains 14% and 5.6% of daily value for calcium and dietary fiber, respectively
