  • First of all,
    We appreciate your support
    and trust in our company
    for the past 50 years.

    I am Chung Yenho, CEO of Dr. Chung's Food Co., Ltd.
    Thank you for visiting our website.


Since 1973, based on the founder's motto "Dedicate to human health culture" Dr. Chung's Food has been trying to contribute to improving and promoting people's healthy culture for the past 50 years.

The journey of Dr. Chung's Food, which began with a medicine to save dying children, has developed to the Soymilk for infants and adults. Also we have expanded our business area to plant-based beverage and food ingredients like soybean broth, vegetable broth.

For the customer's health and safety, Dr. Chung's Food value manufacturing the best quality products as our first priority. Also we have acquired food safety certifications like FSSC22000, HACCP for the product quality, and environmental product declaration to make sure our environment is safe as well.

In addition, we are not complacent about being the No. 1 company that has led the Soymilk industry for more than 50 years, but are accelerating research on new products and food materials to lead the market through academic exchanges with our own central research institutes and external research institutes.

In order to repay your support and trust since its foundation, we support Hyechun Scholarship, The Korea Heart Foundation, Good Neighbors, and Korea Blood Disease & Cancer Association and continue to carry out social contribution activities to return customer love to society.

We will do our best to strengthen customer-focused management confirmed by CCM(Consumer-Centered Management) certification, so that all executives and employees do their best to realize the customer's value of health promotion.

Moreover, we promise to become a company that promotes the health of people around the world in order to realize the foundation philosophy of "Dedicate to human health culture."

Thank you.
